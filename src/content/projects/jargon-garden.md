During a team hackathon at my Ford internship, our team created 'Jargon Garden,' an app that humorously transforms phrases from the user into corporate buzzword gold. Built with React and TypeScript, and powered by Ford's LLM which we connected to through the in-house API, the project was as much about having fun as it was about developing our technical skills. Our playful approach paid off—we won the popular vote for best hackathon project, even though we were the only team made up entirely of interns.
